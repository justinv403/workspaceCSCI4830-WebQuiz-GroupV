About Us

Welcome to TriviaOmaha, your innovative web-based quizzing software developed by a passionate team consisting of Noah Jorgenson, Matthew Konrad, and Justin Vanderboegh. Our mission, as Group V at the University of Nebraska Omaha is to revolutionize traditional studying methods by bringing engagement, competition, and community awareness to the forefront of learning.

Driven by the limitations of paper-based flashcard quizzes, we sought to create a platform that not only encourages competition but also fosters a sense of community involvement and knowledge expansion. TriviaOmaha is designed to break the monotony of traditional studying by offering an interactive and engaging way for users to challenge themselves and compare their knowledge to others.

Our software isn't just about quizzes; it's a gateway to explore various topics surrounding Omaha. From local history and educational opportunities to civic engagement and community resources, we offer a curated selection of topics created by our team of experts. Each quiz is designed to deliver bite-sized information, promoting better understanding and retention of Omaha's rich history and resources.

Accessibility and inclusivity are at the core of TriviaOmaha. We've ensured that our web app is user-friendly and compatible across multiple devices. Our goal is to make learning enjoyable and accessible for users of all ages and abilities.

At TriviaOmaha, we believe in fostering a competitive spirit that transcends mere quizzing. By incorporating a leaderboard system, we aim to inspire users to continuously improve their knowledge and become active participants in their community. Whether you're a trivia enthusiast or someone looking to explore and learn more about Omaha, our platform welcomes you to an enriching experience.

We're committed to providing the best quizzing software available, always putting our users first. Join us on this journey as we strive to not only enhance studying methods but also contribute to community growth and engagement.
